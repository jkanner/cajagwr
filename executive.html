Winter/spring 2018 Schedule

Host Instructions

  • Schedule a date for the presentation. Seminars are tuesdays at 4 PM
  • Collect a title and abstract from the speaker as soon as possible
  • Ensure your speaker finds the auditorium
  • Ensure your speaker is at least 15 minutes early to set-up computer and do sound check
  • You may choose to introduce the speaker. You may want to make a few notes about his/her work history.

Speaker Instructions

  • Plan to arrive at the auditorium 15 minutes early. It always takes a few minutes to set up the projector.
  • The bottom 5% of your slides will not be visible to the audience
  • By default, CaJAGWR seminars are recorded and posted on a CaJAGWR Youtube channel. Please let the chair know if you would prefer to not record the talk.
  • For a high quality video, please remain standing in one location during your talk [e.g. behind the podium]
  • If you prefer to not stand behind the podium, please use a wireless body mic to capture your voice. These are available in lower right hand corner of podium.
  • Please use the mouse pointer, rather than laser pointer, to highlight areas of your slides. You may want to make the pointer bigger in advance of your talk. On a Mac: System preferences > Accessibility > Display
